Method Detail
addMember

public boolean  addMember(Quantity member)

Add an object of type Quantity to the List of member Quantities. The only restrictions on membership are that a quantity may not "own" itself, and only MatrixQuantities and CompositeQuantiites may have AxisFrames. Furthermore, incorrectly dimensioned AxisFrames are not allowed. Correct dimensionality is when the multiple of the numberOfLocations of all the child axes equal that of the parent size. For example, an AxisFrame with "X" and "Y" axes quantities have numberOfLocations of 10 and 30 respectively. This AxisFrame may be added to any quantity which itself has 10 x 30 = 300 locations.
